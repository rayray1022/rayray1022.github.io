POPO

POPO is a curious astronaut monster traveling across the universe. Floating between stars and planets, POPO searches for new worlds—and maybe new friends.

MOMO

MOMO is a mysterious creature born from countless laboratory experiments. Clumsy but powerful, MOMO’s body constantly changes, making it both unpredictable and dangerous.

GOGO

GOGO is a fearless street racer who lives for speed and adrenaline. With engines roaring and tires burning, GOGO rushes through the city as if every road is a racetrack.

DODO

DODO is a genius bio-doctor obsessed with strange creatures and unknown life forms. Always carrying test tubes and scanners, DODO believes every monster has a secret waiting to be discovered.
